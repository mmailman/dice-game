Story

You are a cryptozoologist whose goal is to get irrefutable evidence of the existance of Sasquatch. You are hunting your quarry in the wilds of the Northwest. You must capture photos of and track Sasquatch, but beware for if you get too close you will be Mauled by Sasquatch.

Rules

Welcome to Sasquatch Dice, the game where you try to get a number photos of Sasquatch within a set number of turns determined by difficulty.

  • Select a dificulty and click the 'Start Game' button to start.
  • 'End Turn' and you will keep the photos you have rolled.
  • 'Keep Rolling' and you re-roll the footprints.
  • If you get clawed 5 or more times, you will be mauled by Sasquatch, ending the turn and and photos will be lost for the turn.
