Story

You are a cryptozoologist whose goal is to get irrefutable evidence of the existence of Sasquatch. You are hunting your quarry in the wilds of the Northwest. You must capture photos of and track Sasquatch, but beware for if you get too close you will be Mauled by Sasquatch.

Rules

Welcome to Sasquatch Dice, the game where you try to get a number photos of Sasquatch within a set number of turns determined by difficulty.

  • Select a dificulty and click the 'Start Game' button to start. The difficulty you select determines your number of chances to get a winning score.
  • There are three die types: Easy, Medium, and Hard. Your chances of snapping a picture of Sasquatch are higher with the easy dice and lower with the hard dice.
  • Each side of the die has 3 possible symbols, a camera, a slash, and footprints. Rolling a camera earns a point, rolling too many slashes will result in a loss, and any footprints rolled can be re-rolled until a camera or slash appears.
  • Click 'End Turn' to keep the cameras you have rolled.
  • Click 'Keep Rolling' to re-roll your footprints. But be careful: roll 5 or more slashes and you might get mauled, ending your turn and losing the round.
